# About

Hey, I'm Konstantinos Mourelas — you can call me moure. I'm a technical artist and gameplay programmer who lives in the space between art and engineering: building rendering pipelines, writing shaders and VFX, and shipping the systems that make a game feel good to play. Over the past two decades I've worn just about every hat — electrical engineer, photographer, 3D artist, gameplay programmer, tech artist — and I love that this work lets me keep all of them. 😀

Today I'm a Technical Artist & Programmer at <a href="https://terahard.org/">Terahard</a>, building two upcoming games — <a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/3034660/Dunebound_Tactics/">Dunebound Tactics</a> and <a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/3716320/RuneSmith/">RuneSmith</a>. Before that I was a co-owner and the technical/art director at the award-winning indie studio <a href="https://envysoftworks.com/">eNVy softworks</a> in Patras, Greece, where we shipped our debut Steam title, <a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/2236860/Basements_n_Basilisks_Storms_of_Sorcery/">Basements 'n' Basilisks</a>.

Outside of work I'm usually deep in a side quest — writing small game engines, finding new ways to break the Unity render pipeline (honestly, not that hard), and building and modding custom 3D printers (currently all-in on the Voron ecosystem). The rest of the time you'll find me playing games, mentoring new game devs and 3D artists, and working through the latest fantasy and sci-fi novels.
